How It Started

Nassau County's Neighborhood Plumber

Trailblazer Plumbing was founded on a straightforward idea: every homeowner deserves an honest price, skilled work, and a plumber who shows up when they say they will. That philosophy shaped everything about how we operate.

We're a mobile operation — our fully-stocked service vehicles come directly to you. No waiting rooms, no storefronts — just technicians ready to solve your problem, wherever you are in Nassau County.

We serve Franklin Square, Elmont, Valley Stream, Lynbrook, Malverne, Freeport, Baldwin, Massapequa, Bellmore, East Rockaway, Rockville Centre, West Hempstead, Mineola, Hempstead, Uniondale, Cambria Heights, and all surrounding neighborhoods.

Day 1 — One truck, one mission: give every customer a fair price and a job done right
Growing — Word spread fast. Neighbor told neighbor. Our fleet expanded to cover all of Nassau County
24/7 — Launched round-the-clock emergency service with no overtime charges — ever
Today — Nassau County's most-reviewed local plumbing team, still community-owned and operated
