How It Started

From One Van to DFW's Most Trusted

Marcus "Dr. Drain" Johnson started My Drain DR in 2008 after a decade as a master plumber. His philosophy was simple: give every customer an upfront price, do the job right, and treat their home like your own.

That philosophy built a reputation. Word spread. One truck became five. Five became a fleet of 18 fully-stocked service vehicles and 28 licensed technicians covering all of Dallas-Fort Worth.

We're still family-owned. Marcus still shows up to complex jobs. And we still answer every call — day or night, no exceptions.
